RALLY ON UCHICAGO MAIN QUAD

1100 E 58TH ST, CHICAGO, IL, 60637

11/16/2021 12PM

The Rally

The main themes of this rally are solidarity and campus safety. We seek to lift the traumatized spirits of our student body, raise reasonable demands, and receive the deserved public awareness regarding safety in Hyde Park.

As this rally is political, in observance of the will of those who are close to the deceased, please respect the following principles:

It is NOT Memorial

Separate demonstrations from memorials. Do not disclose any personal information of the deceased as other events will be held specifically to commemorate the deceased

Focus on campus safety

While it is clear that many have a strong opinion regarding the recent tragedy, please avoid digression into layered and complicated topics such as identity politics, gun policies, and political institutions in order to prevent unnecessary confusion.

Be Constructive

The University is providing holistic support to the family of the deceased while contemplating the next steps toward campus safety. It is imperative for us students to be willing to work with the school in order to solve the issues at hand. Please be constructive. We appreciate your understanding.

Preparation — Simple as 1, 2, 3

1

Promotion: This weekend, please forward more information about this rally on all platforms of social media, and encourage all students (regardless of affiliation) to participate.

2

Production of promotional materials: Due to limited time and resources, the organizers may not be able to prepare all the promotional materials, so please work together to prepare materials based on the slogans voted for and posters designed by the promotional team if you are able. We also encourage everyone to write slogans on your masks. We will also provide some markers and paper to write on at the site.

3

Dress code: Please dress seriously and appropriately (white or black), you can wear a black veil on your arm to express your solemnity.
